Autism Questionnaire

  1. Do you find it easy or hard to look at people's eyes when you're talking to them?


  2. How do you feel about talking? Is it something that comes easily for you, or do you find it a bit challenging?


  3. Tell me about your friends. Do you enjoy spending time with them, or do you prefer to be by yourself?


  4. When you achieve something, do you like to share it with others, or do you prefer to keep it to yourself?


  5. Do you have any movements or behaviors that you find yourself doing over and over again?


  6. How do you feel when your daily routine changes? Does it bother you, or are you okay with it?


  7. How do you feel about loud noises? Do they bother you, or are you generally comfortable in noisy environments?


  8. When it comes to textures, are there specific ones that you find uncomfortable or weird to touch?


  9. When you were younger, did you enjoy pretend play and make-believe games, or did you prefer engaging in activities that reflect the real world?


  10. Do you find comfort in routine and doing the same activities repeatedly, or do you prefer trying new and different things?
